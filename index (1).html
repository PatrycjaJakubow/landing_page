Our History

In 2005, three friends who were passionate about technology and entrepreneurship founded a small IT company in a garage. They started off building simple websites and providing basic tech support services to local businesses. However, their drive and innovation soon caught the attention of larger companies, and they expanded their services to include software development and network infrastructure management.

By 2010, the company had grown significantly and had established itself as a major player in the industry. They had a large team of developers, designers, and engineers who worked on various projects for clients across different sectors. Their reputation for delivering high-quality solutions and exceptional customer service had earned them a loyal customer base, and they continued to expand their offerings to meet the evolving needs of their clients.

Fast forward to 2021, and the company has become a household name in the tech industry. They have offices in multiple cities and employ thousands of people worldwide. They have developed cutting-edge technologies in AI, cybersecurity, and cloud computing, among other fields, and have partnerships with some of the world's biggest tech companies. Despite their success, the company remains committed to its core values of innovation, excellence, and customer satisfaction.
